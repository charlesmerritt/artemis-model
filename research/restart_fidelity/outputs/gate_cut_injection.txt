Question: is applying a cut at a barrier faithful, and does it survive restart?

  825.483 / 1179.261 = 0.700  ->  exactly 30% of TPA removed

VERDICT: PASS. External cut injection is exact against native FVS management,
and survives a stop/restart barrier exactly, on stand values.

Mechanism notes (cost real debugging):
- fvsCutNow works ONLY at stop point 2, and NOT right after a restart restore
  ("function can only be used at stoppoint 2"): the restore leaves the stand
  loaded but not in the stop-point-2 context. For the restart path use
  fvsAddActivity(year,"base_thindbh",c(minDBH,maxDBH,prop,0,0)) instead -- it
  has no stop-point restriction and schedules the thin for the resumed cycle.
- FVS_Summary2 emits TWO rows for a cut year: RmvCode 1 (pre-removal) and
  RmvCode 2 (post-removal). Join on (StandID, Year, RmvCode), never Year alone,
  or the pre/post rows cross-join and manufacture huge false deltas.
