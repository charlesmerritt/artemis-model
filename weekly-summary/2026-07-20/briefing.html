FVS restart-fidelity spike & parallel orchestration

branch: claude-code/parallel-fvs-runs · PR #3
Spike complete

The load-bearing question of the week: can FVS be stopped, parallelized, and restarted without changing the answer? A 4-arm experiment (continuous / in-process pause / stop-restart / tree-list rebuild) settled it against real FVS runs.

  • In-process pause == continuous, exactly — the strongest possible result; both candidate architectures survive.
  • Stop/restart is exact on stand values but corrupts carbon (FFE FLIVE is reset on restore — not the originally-hypothesized common-block gap). Carbon set out of scope: config: carbon_extension:false.
  • Management-injection gate PASSED — per-stand selective cut is faithful and restart-safe within a bundle.
  • Orchestrator sketched: bundle-per-ownership, even-flow scheduling. DuckDB adopted as the data layer.

05Open questions to raise

Grounded in this week’s notes and spike results — the decisions still owed.

  1. Does segmentation actually beat the sliver problem, and at what parameters? The Felzenszwalb/SLIC comparison has never been executed; scale/n-segments are guesses aimed at a ~5–10 ha median. research/mgmt_units/STATE.md · NEXT_STEPS.md
  2. Sliver policy — merge-to-best-neighbor or discard? 14,852 sub-2 ha fragments can’t each be an FVS stand; a hybrid/constrained variant is proposed but unbuilt. research/mgmt_units/STATE.md §gaps
  3. Do we accept dropping carbon? Restart corrupts FFE carbon (FLIVE reset on restore); stand values stay exact. Out of scope is fine for growth/volume — but any carbon deliverable needs continuous runs or another path. notes/restart-fidelity-findings.md
  4. What’s the statewide FVS workload & runtime? Unit count is the upper bound on FVS runs; we need a per-strategy estimate and a chunking plan before leaving the five-county pilot. research/mgmt_units/BRIEF.md §7
  5. Which validation reference do we commit to? FIA condition sizes, BIGMAP biomass cross-check, or FIA-remeasurement hindcast — the paper rubric needs one defensible axis chosen. PLAN.md · BRIEF.md §6
  6. Enrich the segmentation stack? It currently uses EVT + a binary forest mask; production wants TreeMap plot-ID, ownership, and terrain — all gated on external-drive access. research/mgmt_units/STATE.md §gaps
  7. Python 3.14 pin vs. environment reality. The repo requires 3.14, which couldn’t be installed in every sandbox this week (fetched from a blocked host); the painter ran cleanly on 3.13. Keep the pin or relax it? weekly-artifact/2026-07-19/README.md
